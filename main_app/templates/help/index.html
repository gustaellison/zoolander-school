FAQs

  • How do I sign up for a class?

    • Navigate to this link to view your classes directory!

  • Where are my grades, and how are they calculated?

    • Your grades can be found here and are calculated off an average off your total percentages on all cumulative assignments.

  • Where are my assignments located?

    • Assignment can be found here, or under the "Assignments" tab!

  • What are the differences in authorization between a teacher and student account?

    • Teachers can do any of the following:

    • Create new students
    • Assign work to students or classes
    • Create new classes
    • Make announcements
    • Create a teacher profile as a part of their respective classes
    • Set up and manage Zoom meeting links, and assign those to classes
    • Any much more!
    And students can do the following:
    • Complete and turn in work
    • Enroll in classes
    • Look up their respective grades
    • Comment on announcements

  • Can I be enrolled in more than one class?

    • Yes, as long as a teacher has assigned you to that class!
