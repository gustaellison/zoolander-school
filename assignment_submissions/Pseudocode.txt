User Authentication:

Allow users to sign up, log in, and manage their accounts.

Dashboard:

Display an overview of the user's teams, players, and upcoming matches.

Teams Management:

Create, edit, and delete soccer teams.
View details of each team, including the list of players.

Players Management:

Add, edit, and remove players from teams.
Include player details such as name, position, jersey number, etc.

Matches Schedule:

Create and manage the schedule for upcoming matches.
Display match details, including teams involved, date, time, and location.

Match Results:

Allow users to input match results and update relevant statistics.

Statistics:

Provide a section for displaying team and player statistics.
Include features like goals scored, assists, clean sheets, etc.
